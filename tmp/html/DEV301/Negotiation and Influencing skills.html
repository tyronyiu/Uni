Objective standards

Some people can get what they want by simply being stubborn. Counter these by insisting that:

  • A single voice is not enough
  • Demand the agreement reflects a fair objective criteria independent from each side's mere desires.
  • Base the terms on unbiased standards such as market value, expert opinion, custom or law.
