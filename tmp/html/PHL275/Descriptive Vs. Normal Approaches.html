Descriptive Vs. Normal Appreaches

Descriptive Normative  
  Non-moral Moral
     

Descriptive - Report or describ what is the case Normative - Prescribe what ought to be the case Non-Moral - Prescribe or evaluate in matters involving standards such as arts and sports. (e.g. criteria for a good painting) Moral - Prescrive or evaluate in matters having to do with fairness and Obligation (e.g. criteria for just and unjust action and polices)
